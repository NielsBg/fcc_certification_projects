Arnold Schwarzenegger

The man who came from nothing

Arnold Schwarzenegger on the Mr. Olympia stage.

A short timeline of Mr.Schwarzenegger's life:

  • 1947 - Born in Thal, Austria.
  • 1965 - Joins the Austrian Army and wins Junior Mr. Europe bodybuilding contest.
  • 1968 - Moves to the United States to pursue bodybuilding.
  • 1970 - Wins his first Mr. Olympia title.
  • 1977 - Gains attention with the documentary Pumping Iron.
  • 1984 - Stars in The Terminator, his breakout film role.
  • 1986 - Marries Maria Shriver.
  • 1991 - Stars in Terminator 2: Judgment Day, one of his most iconic films.
  • 2003 - Elected Governor of California.
  • 2006 - Re-elected as Governor of California.
  • 2011 - Ends term as Governor and announces separation from Maria Shriver.
  • 2015 - Appears in Terminator Genisys and becomes host of The Celebrity Apprentice.
  • 2023 - Releases a Netflix documentary, Arnold, about his life.
